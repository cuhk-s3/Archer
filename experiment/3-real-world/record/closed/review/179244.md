## Test Strategies

### Strategy 1: FPTrunc with Demanded NaN

- **Target**: fptrunc instruction
- **Rationale**: The patch adds `if (DemandedMask & fcNan) SrcDemandedMask |= fcNan;` to `simplifyDemandedUseFPClassFPTrunc`. If the result of `fptrunc` can be a NaN, the source could be a signaling NaN (which becomes a quiet NaN or remains a signaling NaN depending on the target). We should test if `fptrunc` correctly demands NaN from its source when the result demands NaN.
- **Expected Issue**: The optimization might incorrectly assume the source cannot be a signaling NaN, leading to incorrect simplifications or missing exceptions.
