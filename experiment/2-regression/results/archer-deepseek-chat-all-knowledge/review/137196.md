## Test Strategies

### Strategy 1: MultiUseFPInstruction

- **Target**: Test cases where floating-point instructions with multiple uses appear in return value expressions with nofpclass constraints
- **Rationale**: The single-use check in SimplifyDemandedUseFPClass might be overly conservative. Need to verify safe handling of multi-use instructions and potential missed optimizations.
- **Expected Issue**: Either unsafe simplification of multi-use instructions or missed optimization opportunities due to overly strict checking

### Strategy 2: ComplexFPExpression

- **Target**: Nested floating-point operations with mixed fast-math flags and nofpclass constraints
- **Rationale**: Test accuracy of computeKnownFPClass and SimplifyDemandedUseFPClass for complex expressions with multiple operations and flags.
- **Expected Issue**: Incorrect known class computation leading to unsafe simplification or missed optimizations

### Strategy 3: VectorArrayTypes

- **Target**: Vector and array types with nofpclass return attributes
- **Rationale**: Ensure optimization correctly handles aggregate types and per-element constraints for vectors and arrays.
- **Expected Issue**: Incorrect handling of vector lanes or array elements, improper simplification of aggregate types

### Strategy 4: FPEdgeCases

- **Target**: Special floating-point values (NaN, ±Inf, ±0, denormals) with various nofpclass constraints
- **Rationale**: Test boundary cases where floating-point class analysis is most likely to fail or produce incorrect results.
- **Expected Issue**: Incorrect classification of edge cases leading to unsafe transformations or missed optimizations

### Strategy 5: AttributeInteraction

- **Target**: Functions with multiple attributes (fast-math, noundef, etc.) combined with nofpclass
- **Rationale**: Test how nofpclass simplification interacts with other function/parameter attributes and ensure correct priority handling.
- **Expected Issue**: Attribute conflicts or incorrect priority handling during simplification leading to unsafe transformations
