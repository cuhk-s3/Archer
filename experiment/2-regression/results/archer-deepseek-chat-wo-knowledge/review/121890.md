## Test Strategies

### Strategy 1: MixedNowrapFlags

- **Target**: Test all combinations of nuw, nusw, inbounds on LHS and RHS GEPs
- **Rationale**: The intersection logic NW = GEPLHS->getNoWrapFlags() & GEPRHS->getNoWrapFlags() might not handle all combinations correctly, especially when flags differ between LHS and RHS
- **Expected Issue**: Incorrect signedness transformation or missing samesign flag leading to wrong comparison results

### Strategy 2: VectorGEPEdgeCases

- **Target**: Vector GEPs with mixed scalar/vector indices and different nowrap flags
- **Rationale**: Vector GEP semantics with nowrap flags might differ from scalar, and element-wise properties need careful handling
- **Expected Issue**: Incorrect offset computation or flag propagation for vector lanes, or mishandling of broadcast indices

### Strategy 3: RewriteGEPOffset

- **Target**: Cases where EmitGEPOffset(..., /*RewriteGEP=*/true) is used with nowrap flags
- **Rationale**: Rewriting GEP to i8* + offset might change nowrap semantics or lose flag information during transformation
- **Expected Issue**: Lost nowrap information or incorrect offset computation when GEP is rewritten

### Strategy 4: SameSignInteractions

- **Target**: ICmps with samesign flag interacting with other InstCombine transformations
- **Rationale**: Other passes might not preserve or correctly interpret samesign flag, leading to cascade failures
- **Expected Issue**: Miscompilation in later optimization passes due to incorrect samesign flag propagation

### Strategy 5: AddrSpaceComplexities

- **Target**: GEPs with address space casts and different pointer sizes
- **Rationale**: Offset computation across address spaces with different index types might have truncation/extension issues
- **Expected Issue**: Wrong offset type or incorrect truncation/extension when comparing pointers across address spaces

### Strategy 6: SingleIndexDifference

- **Target**: GEPs differing by exactly one index with complex types and nowrap flags
- **Rationale**: The NumDifferences == 1 path might have type conversion or signedness issues when indices have different types
- **Expected Issue**: Incorrect comparison when indices have different types or when nowrap flags affect the comparison semantics
