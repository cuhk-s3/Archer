### Analysis

The patch introduces a new hidden option `-vplan-print-after-all` to print the VPlan after each VPlan transform pass. It achieves this by adding a `RUN_VPLAN_PASS` macro that wraps the execution of a VPlan pass and uses `scope_exit` to perform printing and verification after the pass returns.

I have tested the new option with various scenarios, including:
1. Basic VPlan printing with `-vplan-print-after-all`.
2. VPlan printing combined with `-vplan-verify-each`.
3. VPlan printing after passes that modify the VPlan significantly (e.g., `VPlanTransforms::optimize`).
4. VPlan printing after passes that don't modify the VPlan.
5. VPlan printing after passes that fail (return false).

In all cases, the compiler successfully printed the VPlan after each pass without crashing or producing incorrect output. The `scope_exit` mechanism correctly handles the printing and verification regardless of whether the pass modifies the VPlan or returns early. The dummy passes `printAfterInitialConstruction` and `printFinalVPlan` also work as expected to print the initial and final VPlans.

Therefore, I conclude that the fix is robust and does not introduce any new bugs or regressions related to VPlan printing or verification.
