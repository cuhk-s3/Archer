## Test Strategies

### Strategy 1: Test copysign with multiple uses and different sign bits

- **Target**: A function with a copysign instruction that has multiple uses, where the sign bit of the magnitude and sign operands are not known to be equal.
- **Rationale**: The patch adds handling for multiple use copysign in SimplifyMultipleUseDemandedFPClass. It checks if the sign bit of the magnitude and sign operands are known and equal, and if so, returns the magnitude operand. However, if the sign bit is not known or not equal, it computes the KnownFPClass for the copysign result. If the sign bit is not correctly propagated or if the multiple uses have different demanded masks, this could lead to incorrect simplifications.
- **Expected Issue**: The optimization might incorrectly simplify the copysign instruction to its magnitude operand, ignoring the sign operand, which could lead to incorrect sign bits in the result.
